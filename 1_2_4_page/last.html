Our Suggestions

Cluster 0
Agricultural and Land use sectors

Innovations at the production end such as biotechnology, irrigation technology, and more advanced machinery and equipment

Develop biofuel production potential based on the advantage of having abundant raw materials.

Cluster 1
Commercial and Public sectors

Attract more businesses to invest and loan in decarbonization, green finacial, and environmental protection areas.

Upgrade the energy efficiency of existing historic buildings.

Cluster 2
Transport and Domestic sectors

Invest more money to cultivate people's awareness of CO2 emission reduction.

Promote the popularity of electric and hybrid vehicles to reduce the emissions from private cars.

Cluster 3
Industrial sector

Decarbonize the steel industry's supply chain through extensive collaboration with academia and business

Use new and renewable energy technologies to improve the efficiency of energy-intensive sectors.
