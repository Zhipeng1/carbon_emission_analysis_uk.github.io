BACKGROUND

DECREASE

Between 2018 and 2019, the overall UK emissions fell by 3.6%.
95% of local authorities show a decreasing trend in emissions

TRANSPORT

In total, 36% of end-user CO2 emissions are attributed to the transport sector in 2019.
The transport sector also had the highest share of end-user CO2 emissions in 63% of authorities.

RENEWABLES

Changes in the fuel mix for power generation are seen as a key driver of falling UK emissions, reflected in less use of coal and more use of renewables
